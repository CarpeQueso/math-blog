--- title: Privacy tags: - privacy ---

For my thoughts on privacy in general, take a look at my privacy statement on my other blog. In short, I hate modern analytics and tracking, and I will never track you in any way. This means that you actually have to contact me if you want to give feedback. I won't be watching from behind the scenes.

This site uses jekyll. The source code is freely available on github. The only extra javascript package I use is MathJax, but I serve a modified local copy (not from a CDN).

I reserve the right to advertise in the future, but if I do, it will be in a way that does not track you.
